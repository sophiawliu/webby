Best of soph.ai

Mr. Roboto's best work.

December 4th, 2023

Below are the Spotify embeds of my four favorite playlists generated by soph.ai. You can read about soph.ai here.

I didn't have the foresight to save each of the inputs I came up with that generated these playlists because, at the time, I was spamming different prompts into soph.ai to see which yielded the best results (in a way brute force "prompt engineering"), but I tried my best to recall them above each embed. When creating my own playlists using my brain and without AI, I'm often inspired by some hyperspecific 'vibe' from an earlier decade or era. I documented my very first hyperspecific-vibey-era playlist in another blog post.

Some other character-driven and oddly specific playlists generated by soph.ai that I also liked can be found on @Borzoi Test's profile on Spotify.
