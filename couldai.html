Could AI Come Up With This?

George Clinton introduced us to Sir Nose D'Voidoffunk already and he's too cool to swim too.

July 13th, 2024

Inner album sleeve art from "Motor Booty Affair" (1978) by Parliament.

Yea though he had danced on the one with the rhythm of the Flashlight, Sir Nose remained De Void of funk. Now, aligned with the Bumpnoxious Rumpofsteelskin, this dull duo from the zone of zero funkativity once again attempts to syndromize the Planet, for in the face of the fury of funk they remain cool.

Seeing so it be, Funkenstein renders Starchild this decree: Gather the baddest Master Funkers from throughout the Galaxy. We shall dance down Bimini Road to the Emerald City and do the Underwater Boogie until we get off at the funktion... Hark and Mark forward: Be ye aware of the Motor-Booty... go wiggle.
