"Earth to the Whole Vibe Catalog!"

Whole Vibe Catalog

Because you're living in your own Whole Earth Catalog.

Cools are your tools towards mastering the study and art of VibeologyTM.

The Whole Vibe Catalog is a moodboard curation device in the form of a Whole Earth Catalog spoof. Everyone has their own vibes, aesthetics, eras, and "[thing]core" moments (e.g. cottagecore). The WVC lets you compile them for your own inspiration and enjoyment. Create Vibes and upload Cools including images, links, text, and Spotify playlists to see it all come together before your eyes.

WVC Flip Through

1

Log In to the WVC and see the cover of your catalog.

Page 1, the Function & Purpose of the WVC, is a slightly altered version of the Whole Earth Catalog's Function & Purpose.

Page 2 is your TABLE OF VIBES, where you'll find all the Vibes you've created so far. Vibes are like playlists but for all kinds of media, not just songs. Are you seeing the resemblance to Pinterest, Spotify, and (underrated!) are.na now?

2

Opening a Vibe displays the Cools belonging to the vibe. Taking the place of the Whole Earth Catalog's tools, the WVC's Cools are the media through which Vibes are carried and transmitted.

Open, edit, and add new Cools.

Cools can have an image, title, notes, and link.

3

Now it's time to create a new Vibe.

Like playlists, Vibes have a title, description, and cover image.

But, in my opinion, it's the playlist that makes the Vibe... whole.

Like Cools, Vibes can be edited and deleted.

4

Flip back to the cover and Log Out!

5

Bonus Gerry Lopez Vibe with music. It's a Whole Vibe... Catalog!
